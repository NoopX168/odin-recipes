Description

Chinese sweet sausage is very common in Cantonese cuisine. It has a savory, sweet and smoky flavor profile. This dish takes very little time.

There are several types of delicious Asian cured sausage, but my favorite is the Cantonese-style sweet sausage. It has a red-ish color and it comes in refrigerated packages. This Chinese sausage fried rice recipe works really well with sweet sausage.

Ingredients

  • Chinese Sweer Sausage
  • Eggs
  • Chopped Scallions
  • Minced Garlic
  • Oil
  • Left-over Rice
  • Green peas
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Make the sauce simply by mixing in the soy sauce, oyster sauce, water and sesame oil together in a bowl
  2. Beat and cook the eggs and set aside.
  3. In a wok (or large pan), add oil and chinese sausage and cook for a bit.
  4. Add in garlic and scallions for one minute until fragrant.
  5. Add in peas and rice into wok/pan. Cook and stir until no clumps of rice.
  6. Add in sauce and stir until golden brown color.
  7. Add in eggs, stir fry for a minute or two.
  8. Serve!
